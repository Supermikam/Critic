NOTHING

There is absolutely no copyright information on the website at all. The company chose to display no copyright information. The reasons, I can only guess, could be as follows.

First, the website is powered by Squarespace. Squarespace provides customers with templates of websites. So the design including page layouts and fonts etc. is packaged in a template whose copyright belongs to a third party company. The link to Squarespace is presented on the bottom of every page except for the initial page.

Second, the main contents of the website, which are renderings and animations, are themselves intelectual properties. Their allowed ways of usage and ownership are defined in the contracts under which they are created. And specific terms may vary from project to project. Anybody using the website should have this common sense. For design purpose, this piece of information is entirely omitted.
